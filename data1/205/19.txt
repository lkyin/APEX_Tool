2009-January Sanselan Incubator status report Sanselan has been in incubation since September 2007 Sanselan is a pure-java image library for reading and writing a variety of image formats.The community hasn't grown much in the past six months. There continues to be only one active committer. Participation level is low. A new version has been released.Barriers to graduation continue to be diversity, size of the community, and overall activity. We will address the possible graduation paths in the next quarter and try to evaluate what the best approach for Sanselan might be.

Development.We released Buildr 1.3.3 on October 16th, our 4th release in the incubator. Along with bug fixes and minor improvements, this release featured improved code organization for multi-language support. We also reorganized the documentation to have language-specific sections.Community.In August Antoine Contal has been accepted as a new commmitter and was subsequently voted in the Buildr PPMC.Traffic on the mailing lists has been steady. A Fast Feather talk about Buildr was given during ApacheCon.Graduation.We started discussing graduation in September and requested community feedback on the proposed resolution in October. In November, we initiated the vote from the Incubator PMC and the vote passed.

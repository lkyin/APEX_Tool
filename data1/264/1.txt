Corinthia is a toolkit/application for converting between and editing common.office file formats, with an initial focus on word processing. It is.designed to cater for multiple classes of platforms - desktop, web, and.mobile - and relies heavily on web technologies such as HTML, CSS, and.JavaScript for representing and manipulating documents. The toolkit is.small, portable, and flexible, with minimal dependencies.

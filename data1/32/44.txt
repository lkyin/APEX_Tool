Rya (pronounced "ree-uh" /rēə/) is a cloud-based RDF triple store that supports SPARQL queries Rya is a scalable RDF data management system built on top of Accumulo. Rya uses novel storage methods, indexing schemes, and query processing techniques that scale to billions of triples across multiple nodes.

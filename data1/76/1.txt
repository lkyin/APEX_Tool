Celix is an implementation of the OSGi Specification in C.Celix entered incubation on November 2, 2010.A new structure for the project has been used for the framework. In this structure public and private files are separated. This structure has not yet been applied to all "sub" project in Celix.We are also looking at Apache APR for OS abstraction.

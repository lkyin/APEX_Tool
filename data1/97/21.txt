Description:.Apache Drill is a distributed system for interactive analysis of large-scale.datasets that is based on Google's Dremel. Its goal is to efficiently.process nested data, scale to 10,000 servers or more and to be able to.process petabyes of data and trillions of records in seconds.Drill has been incubating since 2012-08-11.Three Issues to Address in Move to Graduation:. 1.

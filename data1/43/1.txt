Toree provides applications with a mechanism to interactively and remotely.access Apache Spark. It enables interactive workloads between applications.and a Spark cluster. As a Jupyter Notebook extension, it provides the user.with a preconfigured environment for interacting with Spark using Scala,.Python, R or SQL.Toree has been incubating since 2015-12-02.Three most important issues to address in the move towards graduation:. 1.

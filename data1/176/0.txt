Apache Onami is a project focused on the development and maintenance of a set of Google Guice extensions not provided out of the box by the library itself nor the Google developers team, such as integration with 3rd part frameworks or extra functionalities.Onami entered incubation on 14 November 2012.The Onami has ramped up and the project has been successfully.migrated to the ASF. Currently there is a high activity to change code.and site to match ASF branding & licensing requirements. Discussions.on new developments have already been started.The site has been deployed: http://onami.incubator.apache.org.Signed-off-by: grobmeier.

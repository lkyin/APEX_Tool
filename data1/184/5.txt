# OpenOffice (was OpenOffice.org. OpenOffice entered incubation 2011-06-13.OpenOffice is an open-source, office-document productivity suite providing six productivity applications based around the OpenDocument Format (ODF OpenOffice is released on multiple platforms. Its localizations support 110 languages worldwide.
